Welcome to Dr. Lauriane Ribas-Deulofeu website

Navigate through this website's pages to discover my background, my research projects and published works.

In brief, I am a coral reefs ecologist with a particular interest on coral-associated capacities to survive the ongoing climate change and the numerous anthropogenic pressures of our era.
I have a particular passion for the Kuroshio region as the succession of islands from Philipinnes to Japan could facilitate the poleward colonisation of tropical organisms from the Coral Triangle to potential high-latitude refuges such as Japan and South Korea. In this context, Taiwan could play a major role.
