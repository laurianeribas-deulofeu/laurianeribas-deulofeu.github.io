      Overview

      Here, you'll find link to github, dryad and other repositories to my published dataset, R codes or other programs.

      Portraying reef rugosity

      • Overview: Estimating reef structural complexity through the analysis of fine and coarse scales of rugosity using high-resolution depth profiles.
      • Publication source: Ribas-Deulofeu L, et al. (2021) Portraying Gradients of Structural Complexity in Coral Reefs Using Fine-Scale Depth Profiles. Frontiers in Marine Science 8, 675853. DOI: 10.3389/fmars.2021.675853
      • Repository type: GitHub
      • Data type: csv, python program
      • Time period: 2015

      Impacts of heat stress and storm events

      • Overview: Investigating bleaching and typhoon impacts in Kenting National Park (South Taiwan), during the 2015-2016 El Ni√±o event
      • Publication source:
      • Ribas-Deulofeu L, et al. (2021) Impacts of heat stress and storm events on the benthic communities of Kenting National Park (Taiwan) PeerJ 9:e11744. DOI: 10.7717/peerj.11744
      • Repository type: IN PROGRESS GitHub
      • Data type: csv
      • Time period: 2015-2017

      Jeju Island benthic assemblages

      • Overview: Describing the benthic assemblages around Jeju Island at 5 and 15 m depth.
      • Publication source:
      • Ribas-Deulofeu L, et al. (2023) Jeju Island: a sentinel for tracking ocean warming impacts on high-latitude benthic communities. Coral Reefs. DOI: 10.1007/s00338-023-02400-9
      • Repository type: Dryad
      • Data type: csv, jpeg
      • Time period: 2012-2014
