Taiwanese coral ecosystems studies

Discover the high diversity of coral-associated ecosystems in Taiwan. The environmental conditions alongside the steep topography surrounding Taiwan offer contrasted situations for coral communities & their associated organisms to develop & thrive. In the Northern parts of the country, subtropical marginal coral communities are present, sometimes well developed & occasionally bearing similitudes with the Okinawan and other Japanese coral-ecosystems. While the southern parts of the country, including offshore island such as Ludao (綠島), Lanyu (蘭嶼) or the more remote Dongsha (東沙) and Taiping (太平) Atolls present well developed & highly diverse reefs.
However, typhoons, bleaching events & anthropogenic disturbances exposure create gradient of degradations within Taiwan coral-associated ecosystems.
