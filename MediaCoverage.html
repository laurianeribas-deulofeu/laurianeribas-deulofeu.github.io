      Radio Taiwan International (RTI)

      In 2024, my scientific work in Taiwan was featured in a three-part radio series and three video segments produced by Radio Taiwan International (RTI) for the programme “Femmes d’ombre, femmes de lumière” (Women in the Shadows, Women in the Light).

      These French-language interviews explore my research on coral reef ecology, marine protected areas, and the use of 3D imaging and underwater technologies to study and monitor coral ecosystems in Taiwan.

      Content is in French.

      Radio episodes:

      • Lauriane, à la rencontre des coraux de Taïwan
        (Lauriane, exploring Taiwan’s coral reefs)
      • Les aires marines protégées, quelle efficacité pour les coraux ?
        (Marine protected areas: how effective are they for corals?)
      • L’étude des coraux en 3D, avec Lauriane Ribas-Deulofeu
        (Studying corals in 3D, with Lauriane Ribas-Deulofeu)

      Video features (“Décryptages”):


      • Visite de laboratoire à l’Institut d’Océanographie de la NTU (Laboratory visit at NTU’s Institute of Oceanography)
      • Les images 3D de coraux (3D imaging of corals)
      • Le robot sous-marin (ROV) d'IONTU (The IONTU underwater robot [ROV])
